KUALA SELANGOR

Kuala Selangor, on the west coast of Western Malaysia, North West of Kuala Lumpur, has a nature park Talam Alam Kuala Selangor. The park itself is especially well-known for its silvered leaf monkeys, mangroves and the huge amount of fire flies. Personally, the thing that amazed me the most about this area (besides the leaf monkeys) were the excellent possibilities for macro photography: ant-looking mantises, the most wonderful jumping spiders and alien-looking robber flies.
